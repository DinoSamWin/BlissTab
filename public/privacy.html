Data Processing Purpose

We process data for the following exclusive reasons:

  • Cross-Device Sync: Matching dashboard objects across multiple browser instances using your Unique ID.
  • AI Calibrated Empathy: Analyzing environmental telemetry to adjust AI response tone based on work intensity.
  • Membership Validation: Processing redeem codes and subscriptions to unlock premium capabilities.
  • Mood Archetyping: Analyzing local patterns to surface well-being trends (e.g. night-owl work cycles).

Storage, Security & Retention

Cloud Storage

Settings, links, and identity are stored on Supabase (managed via Google Cloud) with AES-256 encryption at rest.

Local Retention

Sensitive records are stored in IndexedDB. Activity logs are purged every 30 days.

4

Sharing & Third-Party Transfers

STRICT POLICY: We do NOT share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing networks. Transfers are limited to core infrastructure (Google, Supabase, Vercel) for performance and operations only.
5

Data Deletion & User Rights

Users maintain full sovereignty over their data. You can erase local caches via Settings. To request a full account wipe from our cloud database, email support@startlytab.com. Requests are processed within 7 business days.

Limited Use Compliant

We strictly adhere to Chrome's User Data and Limited Use policies. No data is used for credit-worthiness or public model training.
