Storage & Retention Policy

Cloud Storage

Account settings, Links, and Membership data are encrypted and stored in **Supabase** (Google Cloud Infrastructure).

Local Storage

Sensitive records like mood, notes, and local activity caches are stored in **IndexedDB** on your physical hard drive.

Activity records and generation caches are automatically purged every **30 days**.
