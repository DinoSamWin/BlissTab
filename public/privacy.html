Privacy Policy

Complete disclosure of user data collection, processing, storage, and sharing practices.

1

Data Collection

The following data types are collected to provide core product functionality:

Identifiable Information

Google ID, Email, Profile Name
Purpose: Used for account identity, cross-device synchronization, and verifying subscription status.

Location & Context

Region (IP), Battery, Local Time
Purpose: To localize search/weather and provide contextually-relevant AI content.

Digital Environment

Tab Count, Audio state, Idle Time
Purpose: Allows the AI to sense your current "digital pace" (e.g., if you are busy or inactive).

Personal Records

Mood Logs, Patterns, Notes
Purpose: Core psychology-tracking to provide well-being reports and detected patterns (e.g. late night work).
2

Data Processing

We process your data according to strictly defined principles:

  • Edge Processing: Emotional patterns and digital pace sensors are analyzed locally on your device.
  • AI Personalization: Anonymized context (e.g., "5 tabs open") is sent to LLMs for creative generation.
  • No Training: Your private data is never used to train public machine learning models.
3

Data Storage & Retention

Storage Location

Most data (mood, patterns, cached generation) is stored locally in IndexedDB. Sync data is encrypted in Supabase.

Retention & Purge

Activity logs and smart caches are automatically purged every 30 days to maintain lean local storage.
4

Data Sharing

Strict Zero-Sharing: We do NOT sell, lease, or trade your data. Access is restricted to core infrastructure (Google/Supabase) for verified technical operations only.
